## Key ideas to borrow

- The page is the canvas.
- Objects are the main UI.
- Interface stays at viewport edges.
- Center remains open and calm.
- Controls are minimal and exact.
- Scattered layout feels curated, not grid-based.
- Motion should feel like exploring a gallery.
